Tiramisu

This super-simple Italian dessert actually benefits from being made ahead, allowing all the lovely flavours to mingle

Ingredients

  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 1/4 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pound mascarpone cheese
  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1/4 cup coffee liqueur
  • 2 (3 ounce) packages ladyfinger cookies
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan, whisk together 6 egg yolks and 3/4 cup sugar until well blended. Whisk in 2/3 cup milk and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture boils. Boil gently for 1 minute, remove from heat, and allow to cool slightly. Cover tightly and chill in the refrigerator for 1 hour.

  2. In a medium bowl, beat 1 1/4 cups heavy cream with 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.

  3. Whisk the mascarpone into the yolk mixture until smooth.

  4. In a small bowl, combine 1 cup strong brewed coffee and 1/4 cup coffee liqueur. Split the ladyfingers in half lengthwise and drizzle with the coffee mixture.

  5. Arrange half of the soaked ladyfingers in the bottom of a 7x11 inch dish. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers, then half of the whipped cream over that. Repeat layers and sprinkle with 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder.

  6. Cover and refrigerate for 4 to 6 hours, until set.
